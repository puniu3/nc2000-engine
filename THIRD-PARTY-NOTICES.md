## Meta team pool (factual tournament/community data)

`data/meta-pool-v0/` transcribes publicly posted tournament results and
community sample teams — factual game data (species/level/item/move lists),
credited per team in `provenance.authors` inside `meta-pool.json`. Sources,
tiers, and per-source links: `data/meta-pool-v0/README.md` (Historia Cup 7.5
report via the Smogon NC2000 Resource Hub and gold.hatenadiary.jp; sample
teams by Beelzemon 2003, Chio, Kitty, and the JP Poké Cup community; Chio's
viability ranking via the ジムリーダーの城 wiki).
