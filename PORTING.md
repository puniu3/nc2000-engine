**M2 status (2026-07): everything reachable in gen2stadium2 NC2000 is ported.**
Unticked entries are unreachable in this format (checked against the live PS
dex): later-gen conditions (`dynamax`, `zacian`, ... exported because the data
table carries them), weathers with no gen2 setter (`hail`, `primordialsea`,
`desolateland`, `snowscape`, `deltastream`), `choicelock` (no choice items in
the NC2000 item pool), `gem`/`rolloutstorage` (unreachable transfer paths), and
`toxic`'s condition block (nothing adds a `toxic` volatile in gen2). The
dispatch panics loudly if any of these ever fires.
